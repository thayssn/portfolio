> me

Nice to meet you, stranger!

I'm a software engineer located in São Paulo (SP), Brazil.

I try to find creative ways to solve problems using the available tools and a lot of collaboration.


When I was young I was absolutely sure I would follow the artistic path.

I loved graphic design, art and photography, even tried to enroll a photography course in college.

At the same time, I've always loved to play games, solve puzzles and learn how things are built.

It was only natural for me to start expressing this creativity in a differente way: through stylesheets, components and a lot of logic.
